Christmas Sausage Rolls

Makes 12 medium or 24 cocktail sized sausage rolls

(ingredients marked with a snowflake can be left out or replaced, if you prefer)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg good quality pork sausage meat
  • 50g onion ❄️
  • 50g dried cranberries ❄️
  • 100g Stilton cheese ❄️
  • 100g chopped cooked chestnuts ❄️
  • 1 dessertspoon fresh sage ❄️
  • 1 heaped tsp wholegrain mustard ❄️
  • salt and pepper
  • 320g pack rolled butter puff pastry
  • 1 egg

What you'll need

  • Large mixing bowl
  • spoon
  • pastry brush
  • cutting knife
  • food scissors (optional)
  • parchment lined baking tray
  • cooling rack
